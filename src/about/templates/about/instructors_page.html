David P. Bucheimer Kwan Jang Nim

Mr. Bucheimer took his first Karate class in 1967 …..long time student of Frederick area Karate pioneer Master William Wickham…….Also studied under Master Dale Tompkins.……over 50 years active training in Tang Soo Do, Jujutsu and Filipino Martial Arts…….promoted over 75 Black Belts in 30 years at CMMA……..One of the longest tenured teachers of traditional Martial Arts in Frederick……..local building contractor and life long resident of Frederick County ………..married to Lisa and resides in Braddock Heights, MD.

Nathan Lowman Sam Bo Nim

Mr. Lowman is proud to call CMMA his Martial arts home for almost two decades. He has been an active teacher for fourteen of thoes years. He has obtained the Rank of Sam Dan in Tang Soo Do under David Bucheimer and has experience in Japanese Ju- Jutsu. Later in his Martial Arts career earning a Black Shirt (teaching rank) under Dr. David Wink in the Fillipeno Martial Arts. Mr.Lowman has designed and taught in a variety of programs and places including Frederick Parks and Rec Center, FCF summer camp, Women’s Self Denense Clinics, and the YMCA karate program.
